Our Story

Arbaz Alam Agency is a full-stack Web development and 360-degree digital marketing company in Kolkata, India that provides digital solutions to empower your business. We help our clients identify your target audience, study their interests, understand their needs, and track what competitors are doing to stand out.

We have team members with IT Background which makes our services high-quality and more reliable. We have a team of 10+ professionals. Also, we have 5+ happy clients and have completed 10+ projects in Indian market.

We expertise in Digital Marketing services that include Search Engine Optimization, Search Engine Marketing, Social Media Optimization, Social Media Marketing, Google Local Listing Optimization, Online Reputation Management, Content Marketing, App Store Optimization, Inbound Marketing, YouTube Marketing, and Affiliate Marketing.

We have worked with businesses from a wide range of industries that include B2B businesses, B2C businesses, Retail, Education, Healthcare, Manufacturing, Real Estate, Food & Restaurants, Travel & Tourism, and Entertainment.
